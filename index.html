SEDANS

A sedan, or saloon is a passenger car in a three-box configuration with separate compartments for engine, passenger, and cargo.

SUVS

A sport utility vehicle or SUV is a car classification that combines elements of road-going passenger cars with features from off-road vehicles, such as raised ground clearance and four-wheel drive.

LUXURY

Image result for luxury in cars The term luxury is used to categorize vehicles that are equipped with better performance capabilities, lavish interiors and all the latest safety and technology features.
